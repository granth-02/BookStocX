FAQs(Frequently asked questions)

Q: Why should I use your site?

Ans: You will find copyright free books easily on our site which you can access at any time according to your needs.

Q: Do you need a subscription to access the books?

Ans: No you don't need to pay any money to access our services and if you receive any mails from someone claiming to be us, that is fraud please report them to us.

Q: Where to access the books?

Ans: A tab named 'library' is mentioned on the homepage. Click on it and you will find a wide range of books for you to choose from.

Q: Are the blogs used permitted by the authors?

Ans: We are providing a platform for these authors so that their content can reach a much more massive audience.

Q: Can I download these books for offline use?

Ans: No

Q: How can we get in contact with the person we want to exchange book with?

Ans: Email address would be available for you to contact them.

Q: Will our data be kept secured?

Ans: Yes your data would only be shared with verified users for book exchange that too after taking permission from the person.
